Event Overview

Tech Innovate Hackathon 2023 was a 48-hour intensive coding competition that brought together over 500 developers, designers, and entrepreneurs from around the world. The theme focused on "Technology for Social Good" with challenges in healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability.

Participants worked in teams of 2-4 members to develop innovative solutions using cutting-edge technologies. The event featured mentorship sessions, technical workshops, and networking opportunities with industry experts.
