ServiceNow Certified System Administrator

Issuing Organization

ServiceNow

Date Issued

May 26, 2025

Category

IT Service Management / Cloud Platforms

Overview

The ServiceNow Certified System Administrator certification validates the essential skills and knowledge required to configure, manage, and maintain the core ServiceNow platform. This certification proves your ability to support daily administrative tasks and implement standard processes in a ServiceNow environment.

Key Learning Areas

This certification demonstrates expertise in the following areas:

ServiceNow architecture User and group management Data management & reporting Workflow & automation Instance configuration Security and access control Service Catalog & Request Management

Certification Impact

  • Validates your ability to manage and administer the ServiceNow platform effectively
  • Enhances credibility for ITSM, IT operations, and digital transformation roles
  • Acts as a prerequisite for advanced ServiceNow certifications
  • Boosts employability for ServiceNow Administrator and Developer roles

Verify Credential

This certification is verifiable through the official ServiceNow certification platform or badge providers.
